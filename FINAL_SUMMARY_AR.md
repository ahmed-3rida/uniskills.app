## 🎨 التصميم

### Light Mode:
```css
Primary: #2E3FE6 (أزرق)
Secondary: #4A5FE7 (أزرق فاتح)
Gradient: linear-gradient(135deg, #2E3FE6 0%, #4A5FE7 100%)
```

### Dark Mode:
```css
Neon Blue: #00D9FF
Neon Purple: #BB86FC
Gradient: linear-gradient(135deg, #00D9FF 0%, #BB86FC 100%)
```

```css
Success: #10b981 (أخضر)
```
